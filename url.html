Uniform Resource Locator (URL)

A URL is the unique address used to identify a resource on the internet. It is what you type into the address bar of your browser to visit a specific website.

Parts of a URL:

  • Protocol: Usually http:// or https://
  • Domain Name: The name of the website (e.g., google.com)
  • Path: The specific page or file on that site.
