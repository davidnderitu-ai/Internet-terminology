Web 4.0: The Intelligent/Symbiotic Web

Web 4.0 is the era of the "Mobile Web" and the "Ultra-Intelligent Electronic Agent." It focuses on the seamless integration of the internet into our daily physical lives through AI, IoT (Internet of Things), and real-time interaction.

Key Characteristics:

  • Ubiquity: The internet is everywhere—in your watch, your car, your fridge, and your glasses.
  • Symbiosis: A deep interaction between humans and machines where the web "predicts" what you need before you ask.
  • Real-Time Interaction: Instant communication between millions of devices (IoT) to manage cities, health, and homes.
